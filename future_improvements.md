# Future Improvements - Dungeon Crawler

## Party & Character Enhancement
1. **Advanced Character Classes**: Expand beyond basic classes with hybrid specializations and prestige class evolution paths
2. **Multiclassing System**: Allow characters to learn abilities from multiple classes with balanced progression mechanics
3. **Character Relationships**: Friendship, rivalry, and romance systems affecting combat effectiveness and story outcomes
4. **Background Stories**: Rich character histories affecting dialogue options and quest availability
5. **Retirement & Legacy**: Long-term characters can retire and provide benefits to future party members

## Combat System Depth
6. **Environmental Combat**: Interactive dungeon elements like explosive barrels, trap triggers, and destructible walls
7. **Formation Tactics**: Strategic positioning system with flanking bonuses, cover mechanics, and area control
8. **Combo Abilities**: Multi-character combination attacks requiring coordination and timing
9. **Status Effect Complexity**: Advanced conditions like fear, charm, and elemental vulnerabilities with strategic depth
10. **Boss Mechanics**: Multi-phase encounters with unique mechanics, environmental hazards, and pattern recognition

## Dungeon Generation & Exploration
11. **Procedural Storytelling**: Dynamic narrative events that adapt to party composition and previous choices
12. **Living Dungeons**: Environments that change over time with monster migration and structural evolution
13. **Hidden Areas**: Secret rooms requiring specific skills, items, or character combinations to access
14. **Dungeon Factions**: Monster politics affecting encounter difficulty and alliance opportunities
15. **Time Pressure**: Limited-time events and deteriorating conditions adding urgency to exploration

## Equipment & Progression Systems
16. **Weapon Mastery**: Individual weapon proficiency systems with unique ability unlocks per weapon type
17. **Enchanting Workshop**: Player-driven item modification using found materials and magical essences
18. **Set Equipment**: Multi-piece equipment collections providing escalating bonuses when worn together
19. **Artifact Weapons**: Legendary items with evolving abilities that grow based on heroic deeds
20. **Skill Trees**: Deep character customization beyond class limitations with branching advancement paths
