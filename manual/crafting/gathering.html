Gathering Professions

Overview

Gathering is the essential first step in the crafting process. By collecting raw materials from the world, adventurers can supply crafters with the resources needed to create powerful items.

General Gathering Rules

  • Tools Required: Most gathering requires specific tools (e.g., Pickaxe, Skinning Knife).
  • Skill Checks: Success depends on relevant attributes (Wisdom for herbs, Strength for mining, Dexterity for skinning).
  • Location Matters: Different resources are found in specific environments (Forests, Caves, Mountains).

Herbalism

Herbalists scour the wilderness for rare plants used in alchemy and healing. This skill is vital for keeping the party healthy and stocked with potions.

See also: Herbs & Alchemy for detailed potion recipes.

Common Herbs

  • Wirsel Herb: Found in grassy plains. Used for Healing Potions.
  • Whirlweed: Found near windy cliffs. Used for Mana Potions.
  • Thistle: Common in forests. Used for Antidotes.

Rare Finds

  • Mandrake: Found in dark swamps. Powerful magical ingredient.
  • Kingsfoil: Very rare, found in royal gardens or ancient ruins. Cures severe ailments.

Mining

Miners extract valuable ores and gems from the earth. These materials are the backbone of blacksmithing and jewelry making.

Ores

Requires a Pickaxe.

  • Iron Ore: Common in caves. Smelted into Iron Bars.
  • Silver Ore: Found in deep mines. Used for magical weapons.
  • Mithril: Extremely rare. Found in ancient dwarven halls. Lightweight and strong.

Gems

Occasionally found while mining.

  • Rubies, Sapphires, Emeralds: Used in enchanting and high-value trade.
  • Diamonds: Hardest gem, used for superior grinding wheels.

Skinning

Skinning involves harvesting hides, scales, and other materials from defeated beasts. These are crucial for leatherworking and some armor crafting.

Hides & Pelts

Requires a Skinning Knife.

  • Wolf Pelt: Common. Used for warm clothing and basic leather.
  • Bear Hide: Tough and thick. Used for heavy leather armor.
  • Deer Skin: Soft and pliable. Used for pouches and gloves.

Exotic Materials

  • Dragon Scales: Harvested from dragons. Fire-resistant and incredibly hard.
  • Chitin: From giant insects. Used for lightweight plate-like armor.
