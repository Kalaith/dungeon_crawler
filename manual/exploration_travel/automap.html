Automap

Overview

While in towns and dungeons, your party's progress is automatically tracked. To view your party's current automap, just select this option. With the map on your screen, you'll note its many colors representing different features and locations.

Map Navigation

Scrolling the Map

While in Map mode, you may scroll the map picture to the right or left should the entire map be larger than your screen. Just select either the Move Right or Move Left icon to do so. You may also click on the left or right sides of the map itself.

Navigation Controls:

  • Move Right Icon: Scroll map to the right
  • Move Left Icon: Scroll map to the left
  • Click Map Edges: Click left or right sides of map to scroll
  • Back to Game: Select this option to return to the game

Using the Automap Effectively

Town Navigation

  • Locate Services: Quickly find healers, merchants, inns, and temples
  • Plan Routes: Identify streets and paths for efficient travel
  • Avoid Repetition: See which areas you've already explored
  • Find Journey Points: Locate exit points to wilderness or other areas
  • Track Progress: Black areas show unexplored regions

Dungeon Exploration

  • Identify Doors: Red markers show doors to investigate
  • Find Stairs: Blue indicators help navigate multiple levels
  • Spot Traps: Green markers warn of trap doors
  • Locate Chests: Brown markers indicate treasure locations
  • Avoid Teleporters: Purple fields can transport you unexpectedly
  • Track Walls: Orange shows dungeon structure

Team Management with Automap

When your party is split into multiple teams:

  • Yellow Arrow: Shows your currently active team's position
  • Purple Arrow: Shows inactive team(s) positions
  • Helps coordinate reunions by showing both team locations
  • Useful for planning split team strategies

Strategic Tips

Exploration Strategy

  1. Check Map Regularly: Open map frequently to orient yourself
  2. Explore Systematically: Clear black (unexplored) areas methodically
  3. Mark Mental Notes: Remember important locations by their colors
  4. Plan Backtracking: Use map to find efficient return routes

Dungeon Tactics

  1. Map All Levels: Explore each floor completely before moving on
  2. Track Staircases: Note all up/down connections
  3. Chest Priority: Mark chest locations for thorough looting
  4. Trap Awareness: Be cautious around green trap door markers
  5. Door Sequence: Systematically check all red door markers

Town Efficiency

  • Visit all merchants (light gray) to compare prices
  • Locate temple (orange) for saving and divine services
  • Find healer (purple) before exploring dangerous areas
  • Note inn/tavern (light blue) locations for rest
  • Identify smithy (deep green) for equipment repairs
