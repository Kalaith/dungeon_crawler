Herbs & Alchemy

Overview

Used in every alchemist's potion, herbs are a source of magical power for the party and are plentiful in forests throughout the world.

Gathering Herbs

While camping, characters can gather herbs by selecting the Gather Herbs option and choosing a character for the search.

Success Factors

  • Area Searched: Different regions have different herbs
  • Herb Lore Skill: Higher skill finds herbs more quickly
  • Perception: Helps spot herbs that others miss
  • Identification: Skilled characters better at identifying herbs correctly

Herb Lore Skill

The Herb Lore skill (WD/IN/DX) represents not just knowledge of where to find herbs, but also the ability to correctly identify and prepare them.

Alchemy System

Requirements for Brewing

To mix potions, the character must have:

  • An Alchemy set
  • All necessary containers
  • All necessary herbs
  • An appropriate recipe
  • Solid skill in Herb Lore and Alchemy helps

Alchemy Skill

The Alchemy skill (CR/WD/DX) governs the recognition and, most importantly, the preparation of potions and elixirs.

Brewing Process

  1. At camp, select Use Skill and choose Alchemy
  2. Select character with Alchemy skill
  3. Choose the recipe to follow
  4. Character begins mixing the brew
  5. You will know the outcome when complete

Brewing Outcomes

Success

Potion is created successfully and added to character's inventory.

Failure

If the alchemist puts together a brew that melts the very jars in which it was concocted, they will fail the attempt, and all ingredients and containers used in the mix will be lost. After all, who wants a potion that doesn't work or a flask with residue of failed attempt on it?

Using Herbs and Potions

Direct Consumption

Herbs and potions can be consumed using the mouth icon in character review. Click on the item, then click the mouth icon to consume.

Combat Use

For faster consumption during combat, keep important potions or herbs in your left hand slot for immediate use with the "Use Item" action (costs 3 MP).

Types of Effects

  • Healing Potions: Restore life points
  • Astral Potions: Restore astral points for magic users
  • Antidotes: Cure poisoning
  • Remedies: Treat diseases
  • Stat Boosters: Temporarily enhance attributes
  • Special Effects: Various other magical benefits

Strategy

Herb Gathering

  • Gather herbs at every camp opportunity
  • Build up a reserve for alchemy
  • Different regions have different herbs - gather variety
  • Use characters with high Herb Lore and Perception

Alchemy Planning

  • Collect recipes from merchants and NPCs
  • Stock up on alchemy sets and containers
  • Brew potions during safe camp times
  • Focus on healing and utility potions first
  • Keep backup ingredients in case of failure

Potion Management

  • Distribute potions among party members
  • Keep emergency healing potions in left hand during dangerous situations
  • Don't hoard - use potions when needed
  • Restock after major battles
