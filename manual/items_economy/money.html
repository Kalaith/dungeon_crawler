Money Management

Managing Your Funds

The game world is an equal opportunity world. All members of the party, man and woman alike, make an equal amount of money for their efforts. This money goes into the party's coffer with each character having an equal share. When your party visits a merchant, this money goes into a community slush fund, allowing the party to pool their money for goods that need to be purchased.

Currency System

The game uses a three-tier currency system:

Currency Symbol Conversion Typical Use
Gold Ducats D Highest value Major purchases, expensive equipment, large transactions
Silver Crowns S 10 S = 1 D Standard purchases, inn stays, common equipment
Copper Bits B 10 B = 1 S Small purchases, food, minor items

Conversion Chart:

  • 1 Gold Ducat = 10 Silver Crowns = 100 Copper Bits
  • 1 Silver Crown = 10 Copper Bits

Party Treasury System

Equal Shares

All party members contribute equally and benefit equally from the party treasury:

  • All earned money goes to party coffer
  • Each character has an equal share
  • Money is pooled for purchases
  • Community slush fund system

How It Works

  1. Earning: When party earns money, it's distributed equally among all members
  2. Individual Holdings: Each character maintains their share in personal inventory
  3. Pooling: When visiting merchants, all characters' money combines into community fund
  4. Purchases: Party can make purchases using total pooled funds

Income Sources

Found Treasure Chests, hidden caches, dungeon loot

Performances Entertainment performances using skills like Instrument or Dance
Services Using skills to help NPCs for payment

Expenses

Common Expenditures

  • Equipment: Weapons, armor, tools
  • Supplies: Food rations, waterskins, torches
  • Lodging: Inn rooms for rest and recovery
  • Services: Healing, repairs, identification
  • Training: Skill improvements and learning
  • Donations: Temple offerings for divine favor
  • Transportation: Horses, carts, passage fees

Money Management Tips

Earning Strategy

  • Complete quests for guaranteed income
  • Loot defeated enemies thoroughly
  • Search dungeons and buildings for hidden treasure
  • Sell excess equipment you don't need
  • Use performance skills in taverns for extra income
  • Offer services to NPCs using your skills

Spending Wisely

  • Prioritize essential equipment first
  • Always maintain a reserve for emergencies
  • Stock up on rations and water before wilderness travel
  • Invest in good armor for front-line fighters
  • Don't overspend on luxury items early game
  • Compare prices between different merchants
  • Use Haggle skill to reduce costs
