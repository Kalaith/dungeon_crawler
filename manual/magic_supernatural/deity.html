Deity & Religion

Overview

A character's deity is very important. Based upon the month the character is born, the deity determines the character's general way of life and may offer bonuses to attribute or skill values. Throughout this world, temples offer respite for travelers, and occasionally, characters in need may enjoy divine help.

Deity Selection

Deities are assigned based on the character's birth month. Each deity has different areas of influence and may provide different bonuses or benefits.

Divine Benefits

  • Attribute Bonuses: Some deities may grant bonuses to specific attributes
  • Skill Bonuses: Certain skills may be favored by specific deities
  • Temple Access: Temples dedicated to deities provide various services
  • Miracles: Possibility of divine intervention in times of need

Worship and Devotion

Temple Donations

Donating money to temples improves standing with the deity. Regular donations increase chances of receiving miracles and divine assistance.

Asking for Miracles

When at a temple, characters can ask for miracles. Success depends on:

  • Whether character worships this particular deity
  • Whether party has paid due respects (donations)
  • Keep in mind miracles are not common occurrences

Temple Services

Temples throughout the world offer:

  • Safe places to save game (no experience point penalty)
  • Party management (add, remove, erase characters)
  • Donation opportunities
  • Miracle requests
  • Rest and sanctuary

Religious Strategy

Building Favor

  • Donate regularly at temples, even small amounts
  • Visit temples of character's deity when possible
  • Make larger donations before requesting miracles
  • Be respectful in religious matters

Using Divine Help

  • Request miracles when truly needed, not frivolously
  • Visit temples after major accomplishments to give thanks
  • Save at temples regularly to avoid experience penalties
  • Use temple services for party management

Ritual Skill

The Ritual skill represents knowledge of various rituals connected with deities. Higher Ritual skill can improve interactions with religious figures and may enhance effectiveness of worship.
