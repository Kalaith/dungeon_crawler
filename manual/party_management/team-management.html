Team Management

Split Team

There are times when adventurers are torn between two or more different quests that call their attention. At other times it may be a member of the party who is unable to travel. Each seems important. Each warrants attention. Not a problem. Split the team.

The system allows you to separate your party into two or more independent groups, each of which travels as they please. Selecting this option, you will see a list of your current party members. Just select those players who will form the separate team.

Split Team Rules

  1. Control Limitation: You can control only one team at a time.
  2. Visual Indicator: Active team members' icons will appear as usual. Characters in other teams will have shaded icons.
  3. Simultaneous Time: Time passes simultaneously for the separate teams.
  4. Reuniting: You can unite teams using the Unite Team option only when they are both at the same location.
  5. Strategic Advantage: Occasionally, splitting the team will allow the party to travel more quickly and infinitely easier than with a full team.
  6. Map Display: In the automap mode, the team you're not with will appear as a purple arrow.

Unite Team

It's been a long hard road for both teams, and the reunion is at long last here. When you've split your team, use the Unite Team option to re-group.

Unite Team Requirements:

  • Both teams must be at the same location
  • The Unite Team icon will be available when reuniting is possible
  • At all other times, it is crossed out
  • Just click to join the two teams together

Next Team

While your party is split, you can easily flip from one party to another. Clicking the Next Team icon, you instantly join the other party. If your party is split into three or more groups, this option scrolls from one to another in the order they were split.

Team Splitting Strategy

When To Split

Scenario Strategy
Multiple Quests Split to pursue different objectives simultaneously
Injured Member Leave injured character at safe location while others continue
Time-Sensitive Tasks Split to accomplish more in limited time
Resource Gathering One team explores, another gathers supplies
Dangerous Areas Send smaller, stealthier team while others wait safely

Team Composition Tips

  • Balanced Teams: Ensure each split team has healing capability
  • Combat Power: Distribute combat strength evenly
  • Skill Coverage: Make sure essential skills are represented in each team
  • Magic Users: Try to include at least one magic user per team
  • Leadership: Consider Charisma when designating team leaders

Risks and Challenges

Potential Issues:

  • Smaller teams are more vulnerable in combat
  • Resource management becomes more complex
  • Communication between teams is limited
  • Time passes for both teams - plan carefully
  • Must reunite at same location - requires coordination

Best Practices

  1. Plan Ahead: Decide reunion point before splitting
  2. Stock Supplies: Ensure both teams have adequate food, water, and healing items
  3. Check Equipment: Distribute weapons and armor appropriately
  4. Monitor Both Teams: Regularly switch between teams to check status
  5. Save Before Splitting: Always save before major team splits
  6. Coordinate Timing: Remember time passes for all teams simultaneously
